Technical FAQ

How does PhilOnSys integrate with legacy infrastructure?

We adopt a "wrap and extend" methodology. We don't rip and replace your existing monoliths; instead, we build API-first microservices layers that communicate securely with your legacy databases, gradually modernizing your stack without operational disruption.

Is our proprietary data used to train public models?

Absolutely not. We deploy isolated instances (Virtual Private Cloud) for every client. Your data remains within your controlled environment (Azure, AWS, or On-Prem), ensuring zero leakage to public foundation models. We are fully SOC2 and GDPR compliant.

Can we fine-tune models on our specific industry logic?

Yes. This is our core specialty. We implement RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipelines and fine-tune open-weights models (like Llama 3 or Mistral) on your specific documentation, ensuring the AI speaks your internal language and follows your business rules.

How do you handle enterprise-scale traffic spikes?

Our architectures are built on auto-scaling Kubernetes clusters with global edge caching. We design systems capable of handling millions of concurrent requests with sub-100ms latency, ensuring stability during peak operational loads.
